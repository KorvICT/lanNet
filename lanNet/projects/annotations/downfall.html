downfall's Annotations

Track # Title Annotations
1 title track "This track will always be fitting at the start.
It was the toughest track to put in.
It was honest, brash, a little spiteful, even."
2 Spotlight "The lyrics speak for themselves.
More often than not, I would look at my reflection and say,
'You're killing yourself mentally, and for what? Someone's approval?'.
Lzzy's chorus on the original is one of my favourite off 'General Admission'."
3 At My Best "People often leave me at my worst, so they never see my best.
They don't deserve it. Never will."
4 i felt that "blackbear's music influenced me more recently.
It's a reminder that I am human, and am not just a rational machine.
Like 'Spotlight', the lyrics speak for themselves."
5 clown "I feel that I act more as an unintentional clown for some people than a person.
I pretend that I'm happy, when in reality, I'm hurtin' inside.
It sucks, but I learned to stop giving a fuck.
You'll see more on the later tracks."
6 thingsudo2me "A relationship that's not the best.
I've been in a few, both as friends and (sorta) dating.
I love the 'Girl, what you doing to me?', it's more conversational that way."
7 interlude "What more is there to say?"
8 eyesore "This has more of a personal connection post-breakup.
I'm just an eyesore to them. One of many songs that fits post-breakup"
9 Sober/Hungover "Speaks for itself. It was something I wanted to say in some cases, but never could."
10 PRIMADONA "I learned people put up a facade. They say they like me, but it's fake.
I don't blame them, they want in on any drama."
11 All I Wanted "I wanted this song to act as a foil to some of the others.
It's kind of a 'sorry, I've moved on.' but more different."
12 Tous les mêmes "This is more for my former friends than lover.
But, they're both at the same level of impact.
«C'est tous les mêmes, et y'en a marre.»"
13 Last Thing I Do "A 'what could have been' - themed track."
14 my ex's best friend "Now, a lot more fitting. Everything became more complicated.
I swear, this was for my ex, and relates to the web I'm stuck in now."
15 tellmewhatuwant "More of a 'hopeless romance' sort of song.
A foil similar to 'All I Wanted'."
16 nothing inside "This was quite therapeutic to sing. I was at the point of wanting to cry out into the crowd. I wanted to get the pain and anguish out for a long time."
17 twin flame "This is exactly how I wanted this to end.
Nothing should end on a note of pain and angush.
It's the point of when my scars would start to heal.
A little on the anxious side, but 'cathartic anxiety' in a way."
